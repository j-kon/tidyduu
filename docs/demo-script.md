# 🎬 TidyDuu 60-90 Second Walkthrough Demo Script

This script is designed for recording a high-impact product video or GIF walkthrough for LinkedIn, GitHub, or portfolio presentations.

*   **Target Duration**: 60 - 90 seconds
*   **Preparation**: Clean the app storage (Settings > Danger Zone > Delete All), then add 2-3 sample tasks beforehand so it isn't completely empty, but keep 1 new task to create live.

## 💡 Recording Tips

1.  **Cursor Movement**: Move your pointer or tap indicator smoothly. Avoid jerky or sudden movements.
2.  **Mock Data**: Populate dates that line up with the day you record, so your "Today" view has active data.
3.  **Video Format**: Record in `1080p` at `60 FPS` to capture the fluid spring and fade animations properly.
